The Duop is a new kind of mop that uses a unique ball-and-socket

The Duop™ consists of a telescopic handle, removeable head and can use a wide range of different cloths and pads making it very versatile.

Quickball™

Removable Cleaning Head to make cleaning work easier and quicker

Duop's Quickball™ system allows you to quickly pop-out the cleaning head from the socket to use it by hand or interchange it with another size

Hypoallergenic

Duop's microfibre pads collect dirt, dust, allergens and bacteria

Use pads wet or dry, with chemicals or just with water. Peel off to remove, wash and re-use

Versatility

One handle, three cleaning heads, a thousand uses.

With three interchangeable cleaning head sizes, the Duop™ can be tailored to suit your needs. Use the small cleaning head for windows and mirrors, the medium for the bathroom area, and the large head for floor areas.
